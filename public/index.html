About The Event

As we head to campus next semester, many of us for the first time, we realize that there will be real challenges ahead. We invite you, the innovators, tinkerers, and creatives, to this hackathon to help solve those challenges!

Join us (virtually) for a weekend of building, workshops, and a chance to win $1000 in cash prizes. The event is free to attend and everyone is welcome!
